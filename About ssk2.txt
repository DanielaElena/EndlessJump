To make ssk2 work you have to copy the ssk2 folder in 2 locations:
1.C:\Program Files (x86)\Corona Labs\Corona\Resources 
	-> where the program was installed,but the ssk2 folder has to be in Resources
1.C:\Users\<user>\AppData\Roaming\Corona Labs\Corona Simulator\Plugins
	-> in the plugins folder of Corona Simulator
